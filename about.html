Family Life

Mark comes from a close-knit family consisting his mother, Patti, older brother, Bobby, and younger sister, Holly. Growing up as the middle child in Texas wasn’t easy, but thanks to tremendous care and support from his grandmother, everything turned out great, not just for Mark, but for them all. Both of his siblings are happily married with healthy kids, making Mark an uncle to his triplet nephews, Ryder, Rhett, and Rory, from his sister, and then from his brother he got his niece, Amelia.

When he was younger, they were able to have some great family trips to the beach where Mark was able to learn how to fish, but they always had the most fun when they took their family vacations to Walt Disney World. Which ultimately led to him packing up and moving to Florida to pursue a job at the theme parks.

“To me, family means the people that you surround yourself with. These are the people that will be there for you, regardless the circumstances.”

Work Life


Mark started his Disney career on the College Program, an internship program for college students or recent graduates. He worked attractions at Disney’s Animal Kingdom. It was a much better job than his first job ever where he was working as a Grocery Bag Boy at Minyard Food Store. Even thou he had aspirations of becoming a professional athlete, or pursuing his childhood dream of being a Zoologist, once his internship was over, he decided to stick around and see what opportunities would present themselves. He has been blessed with many opportunities thanks to that decision. Mark has been part of many parades and shows seen around Disney property. He has had the pleasure of being part of opening up new shows and parades, as well as learning some of the ones that he used to enjoy on his family trips to the park. He also was chosen to get driver trained on various parade floats featured in all the parades. His favorite role though, out of everything he has done over the years, is being able to walk stilts. If you ever get a chance to see Mark perform on his stilts, you can guarantee that you are going to get a great performance. The amount of energy and pride he puts into his work shows that he his very happy he decided to stay here. They even made him a stilt trainer so he can pass on his wealth of knowledge and experience to future performers.

“The joy you see on a kids face when they see this extremely tall person walking around is probably one of the best feelings in the world!”

Love Life

Another blessing from work came in the form of the love of his life, Courtney. He has fallen for her Hook, Line, and Sinker, as the saying goes. Their love has only gotten stronger since. Their song is, “Nobody But You”, by Blake Sheldon and Gwen Stefani. Mark falls in love with Courtney more and more each day due to the love and dedication she has towards her twin sons, Peyton and Neyland, and how they have welcomed Mark into their lives. If they are driving home to Texas to spend time with Mark’s family, taking the boys to the theme parks, or just the two of them spending a day at the beach, you can easily see how in love with each other they are.

“I remember the first time I worked up the nerve to talk to her, we were working together in the parade and I yelled at her from across the float and well, she just fell in love with me!!”

To Sum Up

Mark has lived quite a full life so far, but looking back if he could change one thing, he would try harder in high school and college, and really focus on achieving more while he was there. He does wish he was more financially stable, but don’t we all!

Someday Mark plans on visiting all 32 NFL Football Stadiums in the USA. So far he has visited 4 of them; Miami, Tampa Bay, Green Bay and Dallas! And ofcourse his favorite one so far is AT&T Stadium for the Dallas Cowboys.
