Content Hobbies About Home

Allow me to introduce my roommate and good friend Mark. He and I have known each other for close to 15 years. We have gotten to experience many things while working together at Disney, but have also had many adventures as friends outside of work. One thing about Mark that I want to showcase with this page is to bring awareness to one minor aspect of his life that goes unnoticed. Mark is color blind. He has Deutan color blindness, (also known as deuteranomaly), which is a type of red-green color blindness. To help you understand what that means, all the images found within this website have been edited to reflect how Mark sees them. When you move your cursor over an image it will show you the original as a comparison. Enjoy!
